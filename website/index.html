The Style Thesis

Welcome to the style thesis, your lookbook for the intellecutally inclined, the cozy-at-heart, and anyone who believes the best fashion is always a little bit vintage & nostalgic.


What's inside:

If your perfect Saturday involves a well-worn book, an oversized sweater, and a great soundtrack, you'll feel right at home.

Each page features 2-3 carefully curated looks, each inspired by characters from a tv show or movie, complete with styling notes and a paring of 1-2 songs to set the mood for your next adventure.
